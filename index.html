Respite

The queer humanities journal.

We are an amateur humanities journal with a focus on queer topics, be it sociology, political science, philosophy, photography, poetry, you name it. If it is related to queer people, it is in the scope of the journal. Respite is a journal that was founded by queer (mainly transgender) people who needed a place for amateur queer writing, which is scarcely found.
